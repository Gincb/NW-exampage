Niels Wamberg

About

Niels Wamberg is a painter, sculptor, filmmaker, and radio host based in Copenhagen, Denmark.

This is the online home of his work and a platform for creativity.
Niels is tirelessly dedicated to details - in a good way.

An architect by trade, today he divides his time between a range of croquis sessions, jazz radio hosting, painting and clay-molding, whilst chairing meetings for the most reputable art society in Copenhagen.

If you’d like to hear more about Niels’s story, feel free to roam around this site, read his CV, or get in touch here.
