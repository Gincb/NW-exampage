Sculpting

“Her gælder det samme, som når man skal lave en tegning. Find det, der er karakteristisk ved modellen og som gør hende eller ham til noget helt specielt.”

The same applies here as when creating a drawing. Try to capture that one special trait or attribute that is unique to your model.

Here we have figure sculpting in clay - the figurines being modelled with the thumb and forefinger. Occasionally, with some wooden clay shaping tools to aid the process.
