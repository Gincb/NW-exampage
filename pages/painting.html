Painting / Drawing

It’s really a matter of seconds for one to take a visual snap of a model’s most important features and then jot it down on paper. The colors can help accentuate and create depth. One has a mere 2 to 5 minutes for this entire process.

“Det drejer sig om i løbet af få sekunder at kunne affotografere de vigtigste ting ved modellen og få det ned på papiret. Farverne kan være en hjælp til at få en dybde i tegningen. Du har 2-5 minutter til det hele.”

Croquis. The term comes from French and means simply: to sketch. Croquis drawings are quick - made in a matter of minutes - and usually of a live model. The short duration of this technique and pose benefits both the model and the artist. For one, the models needn’t stay still for a long time and are capable of holding more complicated poses, while the artist can keep focused on the most important elements, ignoring tiny details.

Using graphite pencils for the outlines is a good idea - also ink - but especially charcoal for doing the drawings.

---

“Malerier kan udføres i olie eller akryl. Det er godt, hvis man kan få modellen til at gøre noget, to be in action, og ikke bare stå lige op og ned.”

Paintings can be done in oil or acrylic paint. It’s a good idea to encourage the model towards movement, to be in action, and not just stand around.
