Radio Hosting

“Her gælder faktisk de samme regler som ved film. Begyndelsen skal fange lytterne og slutningen skal få lytterne til at huske udsendelsen. Det hele skal være specielt.”

The same rule can be applied here as with making movies. The beginning has to capture the listeners attention, with the peak-end rule applied, so they will remember the broadcast. The whole thing must be rather special.
